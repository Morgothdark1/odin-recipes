Creamy Blackened Chicken Ramen

This chicken ramen recipe is simple and kid friendly and is served with blackened chicken and fresh green onions (onions may be omited based on preference)

Ingredients

Chicken

  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Broth

  • 1 1/2 cloves garlic, minced or equivalent garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons marinara sauce
  • 3/4 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 1/4 cups chicken stock, divided
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 packages ramen noodles
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 green onion, sliced
  • cilantro leaves

Steps

  • Add flour to a shallow plate. Add cayenne, garlic powder, half of salt, and half of black pepper. Mix well.
  • Lightly season chicken breast with remaining salt and pepper. Dredge chicken breast in seasoned flour and shake off any access.
  • Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot over medium heat. Lay chicken breast in the pan, and cook, turning every 1 to 2 minutes, until each side is deeply blackened. Remove to a plate to rest.
  • Lower heat to medium-low, then add garlic, chili flakes, marinara sauce, smoked paprika, and oregano. Stir continuously until fragrant, 30 seconds to 1 minute. Stir in half the chicken stock and cream. Add remaining stock, then bring heat up to medium-high.
  • Add ramen noodles; cook in broth according to package instructions. Bring down the heat to medium-low and simmer for a few minutes.
  • Slice chicken. To serve, ladle broth and noodles into shallow bowls. Lay some chicken pieces on top, then garnish with Parmesan, green onions, and cilantro.
